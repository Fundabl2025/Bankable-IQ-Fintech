SBA Business Loan

Product
SBA Loans (7a / 504 / Express)

Quick Facts
Max Amount: $5,000,000
Approval Timeline: 1 to 5 days for approvals
Funding Timeline: 4 to 10 weeks to funding
Typical Overall Timeline: 3 to 8 weeks is common depending on documentation and underwriting
Terms: SBA 7a up to 10 years, SBA 504 up to 25 years, Express option available
Rates: Prime + 1% to 2.75%
Payments: Monthly
Prepayment Penalty: None
Funding Speed: Same-day funding available is stated in the catalog
Pros: Lowest interest rates, longest repayment terms, large loan amounts, builds business credit, no prepayment penalty
Cons: Long approval time, heavy documentation, collateral often required, must be profitable

What it is
Government-backed business loans designed for established companies with strong documentation and a proven profitable history. These loans are used for affordable, long-term financing with prime-based pricing and extended repayment terms.

Ideal Use Case
Growing businesses that want affordable long-term financing for expansion, working capital, or refinancing, and are prepared to provide detailed financial documentation.

Why people choose it
Prime-based pricing and long terms can reduce borrowing cost and monthly payment pressure.
Large loan amounts support expansion and refinancing.
Structured monthly payments improve predictability.
No prepayment penalty supports early payoff flexibility.

Unique Benefits
SBA 7a up to 10-year term.
SBA 504 up to 25-year term.
Rates: Prime + 1% to 2.75%.
Same-day funding available is stated in the catalog.
Monthly payments and no prepayment penalty.

Minimum and Key Qualifications*
Time in Business: 2+ years, must show profitable tax history
Minimum FICO: 680+ with 700 preferred is stated, catalog minimum also lists 660+
Minimum Revenue: $25k to $50k+ monthly revenue is stated, catalog minimum also lists $500,000 annual sales or $40,000 in monthly deposits, cheat sheet also shows $250,000 yearly revenue
Banking: Business bank account required, no personal accounts for sole proprietors
Documentation: Last 2 full years of business and personal tax returns, year-to-date P&L, balance sheet, business debt schedule, and ownership details
Credit Events: No bankruptcies or foreclosures in 3 years, catalog also lists no bankruptcies, liens, or foreclosures in the last 3 years
Collateral: Often required
*Minimum Qualifications do not Guarantee Approval.

Frequently Asked Questions
How long does SBA funding take? Approvals can be 1 to 5 days, funding commonly takes 4 to 10 weeks, and overall timelines are often 3 to 8 weeks depending on documentation.
Do I need to be profitable? Yes, profitability must be demonstrated through tax returns and profitable tax history.
What documents are required? Two years of business and personal tax returns, YTD P&L, balance sheet, business debt schedule, and ownership details.
