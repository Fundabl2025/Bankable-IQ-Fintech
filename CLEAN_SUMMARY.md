## 🚫 High-Risk Business Name Terms

### **❌ Financial Services (Auto-Decline):**
Loan, Lending, Credit, Debt, Financing, Capital, Funding, Bank, Mortgage

**Why:** Lenders don't want to lend to other lenders. Instant rejection.

### **⚠️ Regulated Industries (Auto-Decline):**
Cannabis, Marijuana, CBD, Hemp, Tobacco, Vape, Vaping, E-Cigarette, Gambling, Casino, Betting, Poker, Adult, Escort, Dating, Firearms, Guns, Ammunition, Pharmacy, Dispensary

**Why:** Complex regulations. Most lenders avoid entirely.

### **⚠️ Generic Terms (Problematic):**
Consulting, Services, Solutions, Group, Company

**Why:** Too vague. Must be specific (e.g., "Digital Marketing Consulting" is good, but "Consulting Services" is bad).

### **Examples:**

| ❌ Bad Name | Why It's Bad | ✅ Good Name |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| ABC Lending Solutions | Contains "Lending" + "Solutions" | ABC Business Advisors |
| CBD Wellness Group | Contains "CBD" | Wellness Products LLC |
| General Consulting Services | Too vague | Digital Marketing Consulting |

### **Workarounds:**
1. **File DBA:** Form entity under safe name, use DBA for marketing
2. **Rebrand now:** Change business name before building credit
3. **Be specific:** Add industry descriptor to generic terms
