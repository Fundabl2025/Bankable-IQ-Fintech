BANKABLE‚ĄĘ is the institutional readiness ecosystem that turns operationally active but institutionally unprepared businesses into companies banks, lenders, and capital providers actively want to back.

Traditional providers operate in disconnected silos. Lenders focus on transactions. Accountants focus on compliance. Marketers focus on acquisition. Consultants focus on strategy. Very few organizations orchestrate institutional readiness holistically, and none have ever visualized it as a single scored framework. The Bankability Wheel‚ĄĘ is that framework.

1. **SCREEN** ‚ÄĒ Run the free Bankability Assessment via BANKABLE IQ‚ĄĘ. 60 seconds. Instant Bankability Score. The Wheel lights up across the 4 Domains showing exactly where the business stands.

3. **PLAN** ‚ÄĒ Build the Capital Plan inside BANKABLE IQ‚ĄĘ. 90-day Bankability Sprint. Each action ties to a Toolbox Tool and a sub-brand service line.
4. **MONITOR & PROGRESS** ‚ÄĒ BANKABLE IQ‚ĄĘ continuously monitors via Plaid-fed HUSLFi‚ĄĘ telemetry and HUSLConnect‚ĄĘ marketing performance. The Wheel updates. The journey never closes.

### ¬ß15 The 6-Step Bankable Methodology

Every CCA engagement follows the same proven methodology. The cycle is perpetual: Monitor & Progress feeds back into Diagnose.

1. **Diagnose** ‚ÄĒ Bankability Assessment. The Wheel lights up. Readiness gaps, lender-risk indicators, and operational blind spots identified.
2. **Organize** ‚ÄĒ Bookkeeping, forecasting, reporting, operational visibility, lender-facing documentation organized.
3. **Optimize** ‚ÄĒ Borrower quality, reserves, banking stability, repayment readiness, institutional credibility improved.
4. **Strengthen** ‚ÄĒ Recurring revenue, lead generation, growth predictability, brand authority installed.
5. **Prepare** ‚ÄĒ Underwriting packages assembled. Lender sequencing executed. Capital pathways activated.
6. **Monitor & Progress** ‚ÄĒ Readiness continuously monitored. Score climbs. New Focus Areas surface. Lender matches refine.
